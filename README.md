# Back 4 Blood - Card References & Deck Builder

This repository contains organized references and optimized deck builds for Back 4 Blood, specifically designed for high-difficulty gameplay (No Hope/Nightmare).

## Contents

- **Card References/** - Complete collection of all 191 Active Cards organized by category (Reflex, Discipline, Brawn, Fortune) and expansions
- **Cleaners/** - All 12 playable Cleaners with their default weapons, traits, and abilities
- **Decks/** - Optimized deck builds for various playstyles, difficulties, and game modes

## Deck Builds

All deck builds are optimized for **No Hope** difficulty and include detailed guides, recommended cleaners, and playstyle instructions.

### DPS Builds

#### **Precision DPS Build**
- **Weapons:** Assault Rifles (ALL IN on ARs)
- **Playstyles:** DPS, survivable
- **Recommended Cleaners:** Walker (Primary), Jim (Alternative)
- **Focus:** Maximum damage output with survivability - balances glass cannon approach with damage resistance
- **Files:** `Precision_AR.txt`, `Precision_Sniper.txt`

#### **Sniper Build**
- **Weapons:** Snipers (ALL IN on sniper rifles)
- **Playstyles:** DPS
- **Recommended Cleaners:** Jim (Primary), Walker (Alternative)
- **Focus:** Maximum damage output, weakspot damage, mutation elimination, and precision kills
- **Files:** `Sniper.txt`

#### **Shotgun Build**
- **Weapons:** AA12 (primary), Magnum (offhand)
- **Playstyles:** DPS, front liner
- **Recommended Cleaners:** Holly
- **Focus:** High damage, damage resistance, trauma resistance, and shotgun sustain
- **Files:** `Shotgun.txt`

### Support Builds

#### **Medic Build**
- **Weapons:** LMGs (for penetration and mutation control)
- **Playstyles:** Tank/Support
- **Recommended Cleaners:** Dan
- **Focus:** Team health recovery, trauma recovery, extra lives, and revive support
- **Files:** `Medic.txt`

#### **Support Build**
- **Weapons:** LMGs (ALL IN on LMGs for Suppressing Fire synergy)
- **Playstyles:** Support, looter
- **Recommended Cleaners:** Hoffman (Primary), Heng (Alternative)
- **Focus:** Ammo generation, item scavenging, team resource management, and utility support
- **Files:** `Support.txt`

### Specialized Builds

#### **Melee Build**
- **Weapons:** Fire Axe, Machete, or Bat (melee focus)
- **Playstyles:** Front liner, tank
- **Recommended Cleaners:** Sharice (Primary), Walker (Alternative)
- **Focus:** High damage, trauma resistance, sustain, and team support
- **Files:** `Melee.txt`

#### **Explosive Build**
- **Weapons:** Secondary weapons (unlimited ammo), any primary for mutations
- **Playstyles:** DPS
- **Recommended Cleaners:** Hoffman

#### **Experimental Build**
- **Weapons:** Shotgun (to balance ammo types for all 3 gadgets)
- **Playstyles:** Support
- **Recommended Cleaners:** Tala

## Card Categories

- **Fortune (Yellow)** - 31 cards - Resource management and luck-based benefits

## Deck Build Structure

Each deck build folder contains:
- **BUILD_GUIDE.txt** - Comprehensive guide with overview, role, positioning, damage calculations, strategies, and tips
- **Deck file (.txt)** - Complete deck composition with 15 cards, recommended cleaners, playstyle, strengths, and weaknesses

## Key Features

- **No Hope Optimized:** All builds are designed for the highest difficulty level
- **Team Coordination:** Builds include notes about mandatory team cards (On Your Mark, Needs of the Many, Medical Professional, Amped Up)
- **Character Synergy:** Each build includes recommended cleaners with detailed explanations
- **Comprehensive Guides:** Detailed BUILD_GUIDE.txt files for each deck
- **Updated Regularly:** Based on comprehensive guide recommendations and community feedback
