# Back 4 Blood - Cleaners Reference

This folder contains detailed information about all 12 playable Cleaners in Back 4 Blood, including their default weapons, traits, abilities, and recommended playstyles.

## Cleaners List

### Base Game Cleaners

#### 1. **Evangelo**
- **Full Name:** Evangelo
- **Primary Weapon:** Uzi
- **Secondary Weapon:** Glock 23
- **Focus:** Agility and stamina
- **Traits:** Breakout ability, stamina regeneration
- **Best For:** Speedrunning, mobility-focused builds
- **Note:** Generally considered the weakest cleaner; mainly useful for unlocking skins

#### 2. **Walker**
- **Full Name:** Elijah Walker
- **Primary Weapon:** M4 Carbine
- **Secondary Weapon:** Glock 23
- **Focus:** Damage and team health
- **Traits:**
  - **INCOMING!:** Mutations that Walker pings deal 10% reduced damage
  - **PRAGMATIC (Team):** +10 Team Health
  - **MODEL SOLDIER:** +10% Damage
  - **EQUIPPED:** Starts with Frag Grenade
- **Best For:** DPS builds (AR/LMG), team support through health and damage reduction
- **Recommended Decks:** Precision DPS Build, Sniper Build

#### 3. **Holly**
- **Full Name:** Holly Forrester
- **Primary Weapon:** 870 Express
- **Secondary Weapon:** Bat
- **Focus:** Melee and stamina
- **Traits:**
  - **GRAND SLAM:** Recovers 1 Health when killing Ridden
  - **CARDIO (Team):** +25 Team Stamina
  - **TOUGH AS NAILS:** +5% Team Damage Resistance
  - **SELF DEFENSE:** Starts with Stun Gun
- **Best For:** Shotgun builds, melee builds, health regeneration builds
- **Recommended Decks:** Shotgun Build

#### 4. **Mom**
- **Full Name:** Christine Tuttle
- **Primary Weapon:** Belgian
- **Secondary Weapon:** Glock 23
- **Focus:** Support and revives
- **Traits:**
  - **MOM:** Can instantly revive a teammate once per level
  - **INSPIRING SACRIFICE (Team):** +1 Team Extra Life
  - **COMBAT TRAINING:** +100% Team Revive Speed
  - **FIELD SURGEON:** Starts with Painkillers
- **Best For:** Support builds, revive-focused builds, Belgian builds

#### 5. **Doc**
- **Full Name:** Seo-yeon
- **Primary Weapon:** MP5
- **Secondary Weapon:** Beretta M9
- **Focus:** Healing and trauma resistance
- **Traits:**
  - **FIELD MEDIC:** Can apply field dressings to each teammate once per level (25 Health)
  - **SKILLED PHYSICIAN:** +15% Healing Efficiency
  - **TRIAGE (Team):** +20% Team Trauma Resistance
  - **READY TO OPERATE:** Starts with Bandage
- **Best For:** Medic builds, healing-focused builds
- **Recommended Decks:** Medic Build

#### 6. **Hoffman**
- **Full Name:** Howard Robert Hoffman
- **Primary Weapon:** M249
- **Secondary Weapon:** Glock 23
- **Focus:** Ammo and offensive items
- **Traits:**
  - **ALWAYS PREPARED:** Chance to find ammo or Offensive Accessories when killing Ridden
  - **CARGO POCKETS (Team):** +15% Max Team Ammo Capacity
  - **READY TO ROCK:** +1 Offensive Inventory
  - **NEVER WITHOUT:** Starts with Ammo Pack
- **Best For:** LMG builds, grenadier builds, explosive builds
- **Recommended Decks:** Explosive Build, Support Build

#### 7. **Jim**
- **Full Name:** Jim Hossler
- **Primary Weapon:** Phoenix 350L
- **Secondary Weapon:** Glock 23
- **Focus:** Precision kills and weakspot damage
- **Traits:**
  - **CLEAN KILL:** Precision kills increase damage by 2.5% (up to 10 times until taking damage)
  - **SEASONED HUNTER (Team):** +10% Team Weakspot Damage
  - **MILITARY TRAINING:** +25% Reload Speed
  - **RAZOR SNARE:** Starts with Razor Wire
- **Best For:** Sniper builds, precision builds, weakspot-focused builds
- **Recommended Decks:** Sniper Build, Precision DPS Build

#### 8. **Karlee**
- **Full Name:** Karlee Fincher
- **Primary Weapon:** Vector
- **Secondary Weapon:** Glock 23
- **Focus:** Hazard detection and use speed
- **Traits:**
  - **DANGER SENSE:** Can sense nearby hazards
  - **DEXTEROUS (Team):** +50% Team Use Speed
  - **BAG OF TRICKS:** +1 Quick Inventory
  - **NIMBLE FINGERED:** Starts with Tool Kit
- **Best For:** Scout builds, use speed builds, hazard detection
- **Recommended Decks:** Scout Build

### Expansion Cleaners

#### 9. **Heng**
- **Expansion:** Tunnels of Terror
- **Primary Weapon:** M4 Carbine
- **Secondary Weapon:** Glock 23
- **Focus:** Item finding and food benefits
- **Traits:**
  - **SCAVENGER SENSES:** Can sense nearby Food and Cards
  - **PERSONAL CHEF:** More Food spawns, gains same effects when teammates eat food
  - **COMFORT FOOD:** Restores an additional 1 Trauma when consuming Food
  - **HIGH CARB DIET (Team):** +1 Health and Stamina for each Food consumed
  - **I.E.D:** Starts with Pipe Bomb
- **Best For:** Food-focused builds, scavenger builds, support builds

#### 10. **Sharice**
- **Expansion:** Tunnels of Terror
- **Primary Weapon:** M4 Carbine
- **Secondary Weapon:** Fire Axe
- **Focus:** Armor and trauma resistance
- **Traits:**
  - **DAUNTLESS:** +25% Trauma Resistance
  - **FIRST RESPONDER (Team):** +25% Team Bolstered Health
  - **ARMORER:** Chance to get Makeshift Armor from knocked armor
  - Starts with Fire Axe
- **Best For:** Melee builds, tank builds, trauma resistance builds
- **Recommended Decks:** Melee Build

#### 11. **Dan**
- **Expansion:** Children of the Worm
- **Full Name:** Prophet Dan
- **Primary Weapon:** M4 Carbine
- **Secondary Weapon:** Glock 23
- **Focus:** Revive and incapacitation effects
- **Traits:**
  - **VENGEFUL:** Increased combat attributes when others are incapacitated
  - **PROTECT THE FLOCK (Team):** +3 Team Damage Resistance and Knockback immunity while Reviving
  - **DIVINE INTERVENTION (Team):** Random effect played whenever a Cleaner is Revived
  - **LEGERDEMAIN:** Starts with a Smoke Grenade
- **Best For:** Revive-focused builds, support builds, team coordination builds
- **Recommended Decks:** Medic Build (with Mom)

#### 12. **Tala**
- **Expansion:** River of Blood
- **Full Name:** Tala Yazdi
- **Primary Weapon:** Vector
- **Secondary Weapon:** Bow
- **Focus:** Bleeding attacks and Jeff summoning
- **Traits:**
  - **TEAM EFFECT (Team):** +1 Warped Chest and Whistle spawn in each map
  - **JEFF SUMMON:** Jeff can be called by anyone with his whistle
  - **BLEEDING ATTACKS:** Tala's attacks cause targets to bleed (damage over time)
  - **STARTING ITEM:** Starts with Bait Jar
- **Best For:** Shotgun builds, bleeding-focused builds, experimental builds
- **Recommended Decks:** Shotgun Build, Experimental Build
- **Note:** Limited to PvE game modes only (cannot be unlocked for Swarm mode)

- Team effect indicators where applicable
