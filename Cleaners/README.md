# Back 4 Blood - Cleaners Reference

This folder contains information about all 12 playable Cleaners in Back 4 Blood.

## Cleaners List

1. **Evangelo** - Agility and stamina focused
2. **Walker** (Elijah Walker) - Weakspot damage and team health
3. **Holly** (Holly Forrester) - Melee and stamina specialist
4. **Mom** (Christine Tuttle) - Support and revive specialist
5. **Doc** (Seo-yeon) - Healing and trauma resistance
6. **Hoffman** (Howard Robert Hoffman) - Ammo and offensive items
7. **Jim** (Jim Hossler) - Precision kills and weakspot damage
8. **Karlee** (Karlee Fincher) - Hazard detection and use speed
9. **Heng** - Item finding and food benefits (Tunnels of Terror expansion)
10. **Sharice** - Armor and trauma resistance (Tunnels of Terror expansion)
11. **Dan** (Prophet Dan) - (Children of the Worm expansion)
12. **Tala** (Tala Yazdi) - Bleeding attacks and Jeff summoning (River of Blood expansion)
