DYNAMITE - EXPLOSIVES

Difficulty: No Hope
Weapons: Secondary weapons (unlimited ammo), any primary for mutations (ALL IN on explosives/items)
Playstyles: dps

This deck is optimized for high-difficulty explosive and fire damage in multiplayer. Focus: Maximum explosive damage, offensive item capacity, grenade generation, and boss elimination.


RECOMMENDED CLEANERS

PRIMARY: HOFFMAN
- ALWAYS PREPARED: Chance to find ammo or Offensive Accessories when killing Ridden
- CARGO POCKETS (Team): +15% Max Team Ammo Capacity
- READY TO ROCK: +1 Offensive Inventory
- NEVER WITHOUT: Start with Ammo Pack

   +50% Accessory Damage. -15% Ammo Capacity.

    +25% Accessory Damage.

11. PYRO (Brawn - Offense)
    +100% Fire Damage. Gain 3 Temporary Health for each kill with fire. You can sense flammable objects nearby.

12. BOMB SQUAD (Brawn - Offense)
    +25% Team Explosive Damage. +25% Team Explosive Resistance. +1 Additional Frag Grenade spawns in each map.

PLAYSTYLE

Professional demolitionist and explosive specialist. Your grenades and Molotovs deal massive damage (+175% total). Use Offensive Scavenger to find items, Money Grubbers and Copper Scavenger for purchasing power. Pyro makes fire damage incredibly powerful (+100%) and gives temp health per kill (+3 temp health per fire kill). Food Scavenger provides additional sustain through food items. With Hoffman, you have +4 offensive inventory slots and generate items from kills. Your goal: eliminate bosses and mutations with explosive damage. Take down Breakers and Ogres with ease using your massive accessory damage bonuses. Use secondary weapon with unlimited ammo for common ridden, save offensive items for mutations and bosses.


STRENGTHS

- Massive explosive damage (+175% total, +100% fire damage)
- High offensive item capacity (+4 with Hoffman)
- Fire damage specialist (Pyro provides +100% fire damage + temp health per kill)
- Temp health generation from fire kills and throwing items
- Team explosive damage support (+25% team explosive damage)
- Item generation through scavengers and Hoffman's abilities
- Boss elimination specialist
- Unlimited secondary ammo (Ammo Stash) for common ridden clearing
- Team explosive resistance (+25%)


WEAKNESSES

- Lower ammo capacity (-15% from Demolitions Expert)
- Lower health pool (+10 health only)
- Requires offensive items to maximize effectiveness
- Can be expensive (need copper for item purchases)
- Lower personal weapon damage (focus on items, not weapons)
- Requires item management
