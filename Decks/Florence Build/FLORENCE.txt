FLORENCE - HEALER

Difficulty: No Hope
Weapons: Light weapons for self-defense (support role)
Playstyles: medic, support

This deck is optimized for high-difficulty healing and medical support in multiplayer. Focus: Maximum healing efficiency, trauma management, team health sustain, and medical item generation.

- FIELD MEDIC: Doc can apply field dressings to each teammate once per level, healing them for 25 Health
- SKILLED PHYSICIAN: +15% Healing Efficiency
- TRIAGE (Team): +20% Team Trauma Resistance
- READY TO OPERATE: Start with a Bandage

PLAYSTYLE

Primary healer and medical support specialist. Your goal is to keep everyone's health bar green. Use Support Scavenger to find medical items, then use them with maximum efficiency (+90% with Doc). Group Therapy makes every medical use heal the whole team (+8 health each). Field Surgeon handles trauma (+3 trauma per use), and Antibiotic Ointment provides temp health (+20 temp health per use). Experienced EMT gives powerful buffs (+10% max health/stamina/stamina regen). Charitable Soul keeps you healthy while healing others (50% of healing applies to you). Doc's Field Medic ability gives one free 25 Health heal per teammate per level - use these strategically for emergencies. Position yourself safely, stay mobile with Dash and Energy Bar, and prioritize keeping the team alive. Crouch when near teammates (Down in Front!) to prevent friendly fire while healing.


STRENGTHS

- Maximum healing efficiency (+90% with Doc)
- Multiple healing effects per medical use
- Trauma healing capability (+3 trauma per medical use)
- Team-wide healing with Group Therapy (+8 health to all teammates per medical use)
- Medical item generation (Support Scavenger ensures item availability)
- Powerful buffs with Experienced EMT (+10% max health/stamina/stamina regen)
- Self-healing while healing others (Charitable Soul)
- Doc's Field Medic provides free 25 Health heals (one per teammate per level) without items
- Doc's +20% Team Trauma Resistance (Triage) benefits entire team


WEAKNESSES

- Lower personal damage output (support role, not DPS)
- Requires medical items to maximize effectiveness
- Needs team coordination for optimal positioning
- Limited personal survivability (only +25 health, moderate trauma resistance)
- Vulnerable if caught alone or overwhelmed
- Team-dependent for protection while healing
