RECON BUILD GUIDE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

OVERVIEW
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Fast-moving scout that runs ahead to clear obstacles, detect hazards, and 
report back to team. Your incredible movement speed (up to +75% while firing 
with SMG) lets you kite enemies and escape dangerous situations. You handle 
yourself solo when separated from the team.

ROLE & POSITIONING
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

You're playing scout/support. Run ahead to clear obstacles (birds, sleepers, 
bear traps) and detect hazards. Report back to team what they should expect. 
Use your speed to kite enemies and escape dangerous situations. Don't abandon 
the team completely - balance scouting with team coordination.

MOVEMENT SPEED
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Your movement speed is up to +75% while firing with SMG:
  • +11% base (Cross Trainers +3%, Dash +5%, Energy Drink +3%)
  • +45% Hellfire
  • +15% Energy Drink (while firing)
  • +4% Speed Demon (SMG)

This incredible speed lets you kite enemies effectively and escape dangerous 
situations. Use SMGs for maximum speed bonuses.

SOLO SURVIVABILITY
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Breakout ensures you can escape grabs when separated from team (30s cooldown, 
50% reduction from base 60s). With Evangelo, you get enhanced breakout 
capability (+75% breakout speed). This provides solo survivability.

HAZARD DETECTION
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

With Karlee, you can sense nearby hazards (sleepers, traps, etc.). This 
prevents team wipes from unexpected hazards. You can also sense Tool Kits 
(Hack It) for objectives.

STAMINA
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

High stamina pool (+35% + +10% team) and regeneration (+50% + +10% team) 
allows constant movement and kiting. You can run and gun indefinitely with 
proper stamina management.

DAMAGE OUTPUT
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Your damage output is good:
  • +20% base bullet damage (Ammo Pouch +2.5%, Silver Bullets +10%, Large 
    Caliber +7.5%)
  • +10% with SMGs (Mag Carrier)
  • +10% weakspot damage (Front Sight Focus)
  • Total: +30% with SMGs, plus weakspot bonus

You can contribute while maintaining speed.

WEAPONS
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Use SMGs for Speed Demon synergy (+4% move speed + +35% reload speed while 
using SMG). This maximizes your movement speed bonuses and keeps you mobile.

TEAM UTILITY
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Mandatory PT
  → Gives +10% team stamina and stamina regen

With Karlee: DEXTEROUS
  → Gives +50% team use speed for objectives

Utility Scavenger
  → Generates Quick Accessories for the team

RECOMMENDED CLEANERS
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Karlee (Primary)
  → Hazard detection prevents team wipes
  → +50% team use speed helps with objectives
  → +1 quick inventory gives utility
  → Starts with Tool Kit for immediate objective utility

Evangelo (Alternative)
  → Enhanced breakout capability (+75% breakout speed) improves solo 
    survivability
  → +5% team movement speed helps the whole team
  → +25% stamina regen improves mobility
  → Starts with Molotov

KEY STRATEGIES
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

✓ Run ahead to scout and clear obstacles
✓ Detect hazards, especially with Karlee
✓ Use SMG for maximum speed bonuses
✓ Kite enemies with your movement speed
✓ Use Breakout when grabbed (30s cooldown)
✓ Report back to team about threats
✓ Use your speed to escape dangerous situations
✓ Don't abandon the team completely

CRITICAL TIPS
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

✗ Don't abandon the team completely - balance scouting with coordination
✗ Don't forget your low health (+10 only) - mistakes are costly
✗ Don't stop moving - speed is your defense
✗ Don't get separated without Breakout available (30s cooldown)
✗ Don't ignore hazards, especially with Karlee's detection

SUMMARY
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

You're the fast-moving scout. Run ahead to clear obstacles, detect hazards, and 
report back to team. Use your incredible movement speed to kite enemies and 
escape dangerous situations. Breakout provides solo survivability when 
separated. With Karlee, hazard detection prevents team wipes. Your goal is 
clearing the path, detecting threats, and handling yourself solo when needed, 
while staying coordinated with the team.
