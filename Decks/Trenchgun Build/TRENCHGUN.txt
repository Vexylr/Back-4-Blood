TRENCHGUN - SHOTGUN

Difficulty: No Hope
Weapons: AA12 (primary), Magnum (offhand)
Playstyles: dps, front liner

This deck is optimized for high-difficulty shotgun gameplay using the AA12. Focus: High damage, damage resistance, trauma resistance, and shotgun sustain.


RECOMMENDED CLEANERS

PRIMARY: HENG
- SCAVENGER SENSES: Can sense nearby Food and Cards
- PERSONAL CHEF: More Food spawns, gains same effects when teammates eat food
- COMFORT FOOD: Restores an additional 1 Trauma when consuming Food
- HIGH CARB DIET (Team): +1 Health and Stamina for each Food consumed
- I.E.D: Start with Pipe Bomb
- Note: Available in Tunnels of Terror expansion


DECK COMPOSITION (15 cards)

1. BUCKSHOT BRUISER (Brawn - Defense)
   When using Shotguns, gain 0.25 Temporary Health for each pellet that hits.

2. HUNKER DOWN (Discipline - Defense)
   While crouching: +10% Damage Resistance and +40% Accuracy.

3. SCATTERGUN SKILLS (Discipline - Offense)
   +40% Reload Speed with Shotguns.

4. SHELL CARRIER (Fortune - Offense)
   +30% Shotgun Ammo Capacity. +10% Damage with Shotguns.

5. ROLLING THUNDER (Reflex - Mobility)
   +35% Movement Speed while firing with Shotguns. +10% Damage with Shotguns.

6. AMPED UP (Brawn - Defense)
   When a horde is triggered, your team gains 20 Health.

7. FRESH BANDAGE (Brawn - Defense)
   At the start of each level: recover 15 Trauma Damage, restore missing Health.

8. WOODEN ARMOR (Brawn - Defense)
   +30% Trauma Resistance. -100% Fire Resistance.

9. BODY ARMOR (Brawn - Defense)
   +20% Trauma Resistance.

10. MOTORCYCLE HELMET (Discipline - Defense)
   +15% Damage Resistance. +10 Health. DISABLES: Aim Down Sights.

11. MAG COUPLER (Reflex - Offense)
    +50% Reload Speed. DISABLES: Aim Down Sights.

12. QUICK KILL (Reflex - Offense)
    +50% Accuracy. DISABLES: Aim Down Sights.

13. AMMO SCAVENGER (Discipline - Utility)
    You can sense nearby Ammo. 1 Additional Ammo crate spawn.

14. DOWN IN FRONT! (Discipline - Defense)
    While crouching you neither take nor deal Friendly Fire damage. +10 Health.

15. FOOD SCAVENGER (Fortune - Utility)
    You can sense nearby Food Items. Adds a moderate amount of Food Items to the world. Food Items restore an additional 3 Health.


TOTAL + BUFFS SUMMARY

BASE DECK:
- Health: +20 (+10 Down in Front! + +10 Motorcycle Helmet)
- Damage: +35% base (+25% Glass Cannon + +10% Shell Carrier) + +10% while firing (Rolling Thunder)
- Shotgun Damage: +20% (+10% Shell Carrier + +10% Rolling Thunder)
- Shotgun Ammo Capacity: +30% (Shell Carrier)
- Trauma Resistance: +50% (Wooden Armor + Body Armor)
- Damage Resistance: +15% base (Motorcycle Helmet) + +10% while crouching (Hunker Down) = +25% total while crouched
- Fire Resistance: -100% (Wooden Armor)
- Reload Speed: +90% (+50% Mag Coupler + +40% Scattergun Skills)
- Accuracy: +50% base (Quick Kill) + +40% while crouching (Hunker Down) = +90% total while crouching
- Move Speed: +35% while firing with Shotguns (Rolling Thunder)

WITH HENG:
- Food Synergy: More Food spawns + gains effects when teammates eat + +1 Trauma when consuming
- Team Benefits: +1 Health and Stamina for each Food consumed
- Food Detection: Can sense nearby Food
- Starts with: Pipe Bomb


PLAYSTYLE

Shotgun specialist focusing on AA12 with hip-fire accuracy. High trauma resistance (50%) and sustain cards keep you durable. AMMO SCAVENGER added for ammo sustain. Shell Carrier provides +10% shotgun damage and +30% shotgun ammo capacity. Rolling Thunder gives +10% shotgun damage and +35% movement speed while firing. Scattergun Skills provides +40% shotgun reload speed, combined with Mag Coupler for +90% total reload speed. Crouch when firing for better pellet grouping (Hunker Down provides +40% accuracy and +10% damage resistance while crouched). Buckshot Bruiser provides temp health from shotgun hits (0.25 per pellet). Amped Up provides team healing during hordes. Fresh Bandage provides trauma/health recovery at level start. Food Scavenger synergizes with Heng for trauma management. Use Magnum as offhand for mutations.


STRENGTHS

- High damage output (+35% base + +10% while firing = +45% total potential)
- Excellent trauma resistance (50%)
- Good sustain through Buckshot Bruiser temp health, food, and team healing
- Very fast reload speed (+90% total)
- High hip-fire accuracy (+50% base, +90% while crouching)
- Movement speed while firing (+35%) for kiting
- Shotgun ammo capacity (+30%)
- With Heng: Enhanced food synergy and food sensing


WEAKNESSES

- Low health pool (-20% net)
- No fire resistance (-100%)
- Needs to find AA12 early for optimal performance
- DISABLES: Aim Down Sights (hip-fire only)
- Requires crouching for optimal defense
