EXPLOSIVE BUILD GUIDE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

OVERVIEW
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Professional demolitionist and explosive specialist. Your grenades and Molotovs 
deal massive damage (+175% total). Your job is eliminating bosses and mutations 
with explosive damage. You're the boss elimination specialist.

ROLE & POSITIONING
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

You're playing DPS. Use your offensive items (grenades, Molotovs) to deal 
massive damage to bosses and mutations. Save offensive items for important 
targets - use your secondary weapon with unlimited ammo for common ridden. With 
Hoffman, you have +4 offensive inventory slots.

EXPLOSIVE DAMAGE
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Your explosive damage is +175% total from:
  • Demolitions Expert (+50%)
  • Grenade Training (+25%)
  • Bomb Squad (+25% team)
  • Pyro (+100% fire damage)

This makes your grenades and Molotovs incredibly powerful. Combined with Pyro's 
+100% fire damage, Molotovs are especially devastating.

ITEM GENERATION
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Use Offensive Scavenger to find items. Money Grubbers and Copper Scavenger 
provide purchasing power. Highwayman gives 5% chance to spawn ammo/Molotov/Bait 
Jar on secondary kills. With Hoffman, Always Prepared generates items from 
kills. This ensures you have items available.

ITEM CAPACITY
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

With Hoffman, you have +4 offensive inventory slots (+3 base + 1 Ready to Rock). 
This means more items available per level. Belt Clip also gives +1 quick slot 
inventory.

SUSTAIN
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Pyro
  → Gives +3 temp health per fire kill

Food Scavenger
  → Provides additional sustain through food items

This provides good survivability.

FIRE DAMAGE SPECIALIST
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Pyro makes fire damage incredibly powerful (+100%). Molotovs become especially 
effective. You can sense flammable objects nearby, which helps with positioning 
fire attacks.

TEAM SUPPORT
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Bomb Squad
  → Gives +25% team explosive damage and +25% team explosive resistance
  → This helps the entire team when using explosives
  → +1 Frag Grenade spawns per map provides additional utility

WEAPONS
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Use secondary weapon with unlimited ammo (Ammo Stash) for common ridden 
clearing. Save your primary weapon for mutations, but focus on using offensive 
items for damage.

RECOMMENDED CLEANER
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Hoffman
  → +4 offensive inventory slots means more items available
  → +15% team ammo capacity helps mitigate Demolitions Expert's -15% ammo 
    penalty
  → Always Prepared generates items from kills
  → Starts with Ammo Pack for team utility

KEY STRATEGIES
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

✓ Use Offensive Scavenger to find items
✓ Collect copper for item purchases (Copper Scavenger, Money Grubbers)
✓ Save offensive items for bosses and mutations
✓ Use secondary weapon with unlimited ammo for common ridden
✓ Use Pyro for fire damage (+100% fire damage)
✓ Use Food Scavenger for additional sustain
✓ Take down bosses and mutations with explosive damage
✓ Communicate when you're using items for coordination

CRITICAL TIPS
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

✗ Don't waste offensive items on common ridden - use secondary weapon
✗ Don't ignore item generation - you need to find/purchase items
✗ Don't forget your low health pool - stay safe
✗ Don't ignore copper management - you need copper for item purchases

SUMMARY
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

You're the explosive specialist. Use your massive explosive damage (+175%) to 
eliminate bosses and mutations efficiently. With Hoffman, you have +4 offensive 
inventory slots and generate items from kills. Your goal is taking down Breakers 
and Ogres with ease using your explosive damage bonuses. Use secondary weapon 
for common ridden, save offensive items for important targets.
