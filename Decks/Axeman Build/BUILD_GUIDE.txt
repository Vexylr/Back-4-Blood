AXEMAN BUILD GUIDE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

OVERVIEW
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Frontline melee specialist using Fire Axe or Claws. Focused on melee damage, 
cleave, and team healing through kills. With Sharice, you have the best trauma 
resistance possible (55%). Your job is clearing common ridden, healing 
teammates, and providing crowd control.

ROLE & POSITIONING
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

You're playing frontliner/tank. Get in front of your team, crouch when near 
them with Down in Front!, and clear common ridden with your melee weapon. Mean 
Drunk gives you cleave - your attacks hit multiple enemies in a large area. 
Vanguard heals teammates when you kill (+1 health to all within 10 meters).

Crouch when near teammates with Down in Front! to prevent friendly fire. You'll 
be in front clearing common ridden, so position yourself where you can protect 
your team while staying within Vanguard range (10 meters) to heal them with 
your kills.

WEAPONS
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Fire Axe or Claws
  → Mean Drunk gives you cleave - your attacks hit multiple enemies, perfect 
    for hordes
  → Meth Head stacks build quickly - 10 swings gets you max stacks for +50% 
    speed and stamina efficiency

CROWD CONTROL
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Heavy Hitter
  → Makes your first melee hit against each target always Stumble them
  → Provides excellent crowd control, especially useful for mutations

SUSTAIN
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Battle Lust
  → Heals you +1 health and +0.125 trauma per melee kill

Vanguard
  → Heals you AND teammates within 10 meters +1 health per melee kill
  → This healing is active from your very first kill, so stay aggressive and 
    keep killing

TRAUMA RESISTANCE
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Base: +30% from Durable and Fanny Pack

With Sharice: +55% total - the best possible trauma resistance for melee builds

This makes you very durable on high difficulties.

RECOMMENDED CLEANER
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Sharice
  → +55% trauma resistance total - the best possible for melee builds
  → Makeshift Armor generation from knocked armor synergizes with your 
    offensive inventory
  → Starts with Fire Axe and Flash Bang
  → Note: Requires Tunnels of Terror expansion

KEY STRATEGIES
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

✓ Get your melee weapon early - Fire Axe or Claws
✓ Crouch when near teammates
✓ Clear common ridden aggressively
✓ Stack Meth Head quickly - 10 swings gets you max
✓ Stay within 10 meters of teammates to heal them with Vanguard
✓ Use Heavy Hitter for crowd control (first-hit stumbles)
✓ Use food for additional sustain
✓ Stay aggressive - your sustain depends on kills

CRITICAL TIPS
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

✗ Don't forget to crouch - Down in Front! prevents friendly fire
✗ Don't abandon your team - you heal them with kills
✗ Don't waste time - start swinging immediately to build stacks
✗ Don't get separated from team - you need to be within 10 meters to heal them
✗ Don't ignore mutations - Heavy Hitter helps with crowd control

SUMMARY
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

You're a frontline melee specialist. Get in front, crouch when near teammates, 
clear hordes with cleave damage, and heal your team through kills. Heavy Hitter 
provides crowd control with first-hit stumbles. With Sharice, you have the best 
trauma resistance possible (55%) for melee builds. Stay aggressive and keep 
killing to maintain sustain.
