AXEMAN - MELEE

Difficulty: No Hope
Weapons: Fire Axe or Claws (melee focus)
Playstyles: front liner, tank

This deck is optimized for high-difficulty melee combat in multiplayer. Focus: Melee damage, cleave, healing on kills, trauma resistance, and team support through melee.


RECOMMENDED CLEANERS

PRIMARY: SHARICE
- DAUNTLESS: +25% Trauma Resistance
- FIRST RESPONDER (Team): +25% Team Bolstered Health
- ARMORER: Armor knocked off Ridden has chance to become Makeshift Armor
- STUNNER: Starts with Flash Bang
- Starts with Fire Axe
- Note: Available in Tunnels of Terror expansion


DECK COMPOSITION (15 cards)

1. SLUGGER (Reflex - Offense)
   +5 Health. +10% Melee Efficiency. +20% Melee Attack Speed.

2. METH HEAD (Reflex - Offense)
   Every Melee swing grants 5% Melee Attack Speed and 5% Melee Stamina Efficiency for 6 seconds, stacking up to 10 times. Your Melee Attacks no longer stick to tough enemies. DISABLES: Aim Down Sights.

3. BRAZEN (Reflex - Offense)
   +15% Melee Stamina Efficiency. +20% Melee Attack Speed.

4. VANGUARD (Discipline - Offense)
   Melee kills heal 1 Health to yourself and teammates within 10 meters.

5. DOWN IN FRONT! (Discipline - Defense)
   While crouching you neither take nor deal Friendly Fire damage. +10 Health.

6. AMPED UP (Brawn - Defense)
   When a horde is triggered, your team gains 20 Health.

7. MEAN DRUNK (Brawn - Offense)
   +40% Melee Damage. Your Melee Attacks cause cleave through enemies.

8. SPIKY BITS (Brawn - Offense)
   +15 Melee Damage. +20% Damage Resistance while using a Melee Weapon. -15% Ammo Capacity.

9. DURABLE (Brawn - Defense)
   +15% Trauma Resistance. +10 Health. +20% Fire Resistance.

10. HEAVY HITTER (Brawn - Offense)
    Melee hits always Stumble Ridden the first time they are hit (once per target).

11. BATTLE LUST (Brawn - Defense)
    Melee kills heal 1 Health and 0.125 Trauma.

12. FANNY PACK (Reflex - Utility)
    +1 Support Inventory. +15% Trauma Resistance.

13. AMMO SCAVENGER (Discipline - Utility)
    You can sense nearby Ammo. 1 Additional Ammo crate spawn.

14. FOOD SCAVENGER (Fortune - Utility)
    You can sense nearby Food Items. Adds a moderate amount of Food Items to the world. Food Items restore an additional 3 Health.

15. WELL RESTED (Brawn - Utility)
    +20% Team Overheal. +5% Team Healing Efficiency.


TOTAL + BUFFS SUMMARY

BASE DECK:
- Health: +15
- Melee Damage: +40% (Mean Drunk) + 15 Flat (Spiky Bits)
- Melee Attack Speed: +40% base (Brazen + Slugger) + up to +50% (Meth Head stacks)
- Melee Stamina Efficiency: +25% base + up to +50% (Meth Head stacks)
- Trauma Resistance: +30% (Durable + Fanny Pack)
- Damage Resistance: +20% while using Melee Weapon (Spiky Bits)
- Fire Resistance: +20% (Durable)
- Support Inventory: +1
- Offensive Inventory: +2

WITH SHARICE:
- Trauma Resistance: +55% (+30% base + 25% Dauntless)
- Team Bolstered Health: +25%
- Makeshift Armor: Chance from knocked armor
- Starts with: Fire Axe + Flash Bang


PLAYSTYLE

Frontline melee specialist using Fire Axe or Claws. Crouch when near teammates (Down in Front!) to prevent friendly fire. Mean Drunk provides cleave for AOE damage, handling hordes efficiently. Meth Head stacks build quickly (10 swings = max stacks). Vanguard heals teammates when you kill (+1 health to all within 10m), and Battle Lust heals you (+1 health + 0.125 trauma per kill). Heavy Hitter provides crowd control with first-hit stumbles. Use Food Scavenger for additional sustain. With Sharice, you have +55% trauma resistance and Makeshift Armor generation.


STRENGTHS

- High melee damage (+40% + 15 flat)
- Cleave damage handles hordes efficiently
- Team healing through Vanguard
- Personal sustain through Battle Lust
- Good trauma resistance (30%, 55% with Sharice)
- Crowd control with Heavy Hitter
- Fast attack speed when Meth Head stacked (+90% total)
- High stamina efficiency when stacked (+75% total)
- Food sustain through Food Scavenger
- With Sharice: Best trauma resistance possible (55%) + Makeshift Armor generation


WEAKNESSES

- Melee-only (limited range capability)
- Requires good positioning and map knowledge
- Vulnerable to mutations if caught alone
- Needs team support for ranged threats
- Lower health pool (+15 health only)
- Requires maintaining Meth Head stacks for optimal performance
