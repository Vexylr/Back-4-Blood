SAWED OFF BUILD GUIDE

Belgian shotgun specialist with unlimited ammo (Belgian is a secondary weapon). Extremely tanky with 80% trauma resistance and +35% damage resistance while crouched. Your job is using the Belgian for unlimited ammo while maintaining high survivability through tankiness and sustain.

You're playing DPS/frontliner. Use the Belgian as your secondary weapon - it has unlimited ammo through Ammo Stash. Crouch when firing for optimal defense (+35% damage resistance total while crouched). Your extremely high trauma resistance (80%) makes you very durable despite the close-range nature of the Belgian.

Crouch when firing and when near teammates. Hunker Down provides +40% accuracy and +10% damage resistance while crouched, and you already have +25% base damage resistance from Padded Suit and Motorcycle Helmet for +35% total while crouched. This makes you very tanky.

Use Belgian as secondary (unlimited ammo). Use any primary weapon for mutations - you're focused on the Belgian for common ridden clearing. You can't aim down sights (Mag Coupler, Motorcycle Helmet disable ADS), so hip-fire only.

Ammo Stash gives unlimited ammo to secondary weapons. The Belgian reloads 20% slower base, but you have +130% reload speed total from Scattergun Skills, Mag Coupler, and Widemouth Magwell, so reloading is still very fast.

Buckshot Bruiser provides temp health from shotgun hits - 0.25 per pellet, and the Belgian fires 12 pellets, so that's 3 temp health per hit if all connect. Food Scavenger synergizes with Heng for trauma management. Fit as a Fiddle gives +20% team bolstered health and +5 team health.

You have 80% trauma resistance from Wooden Armor, Body Armor, Durable, and Fanny Pack - the best possible trauma resistance in the game. This makes you incredibly durable on high difficulties.

You have +25% base damage resistance from Padded Suit and Motorcycle Helmet, plus +10% while crouching from Hunker Down, for +35% total while crouched. You get +40% accuracy while crouching from Hunker Down. Combined with +25 health and 80% trauma resistance, you're very tanky.

With Heng, food synergy is excellent. More Food spawns, you gain effects when teammates eat food, and food restores +1 trauma when you consume it. Your team gains +1 health and stamina per food consumed. This provides excellent sustain.

Use Belgian as secondary (unlimited ammo). Crouch when firing for optimal defense (+35% damage resistance). Use primary weapon for mutations. Use food for sustain, especially with Heng. Crouch when near teammates. Reload quickly with +130% reload speed. Stay in close range - Belgian is a close-range weapon.

Don't try to aim down sights - you can't, it's hip-fire only. Don't forget to crouch - optimal defense requires crouching. Don't waste food - it's important for sustain with Heng. Don't forget fire resistance is negative (-80% net) - avoid fire damage. Don't play at long range - Belgian is close-range.
