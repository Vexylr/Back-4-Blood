TESLA - EXPERIMENTAL
=======================

Difficulty: No Hope

Weapons: LMGs or Snipers (for Suppressing Fire utility and ammo efficiency). Prioritize weapons with good ammo economy since gadgets consume ammo heavily.

Playstyles: support

This deck is optimized for high-difficulty gameplay using experimental DLC technology in multiplayer.
Focus: Experimental gadgets, ammo generation, team buffs, and advanced tech utilization.

RECOMMENDED CLEANERS:
---------------------

PRIMARY RECOMMENDATION: EVANGELO
- BORN SURVIVOR: Can break out of grabs, +75% Breakout Speed
- LIGHT FOOTED (Team): +5% Team Movement Speed
- FIT: +25% Stamina Regen
- FIRESTARTER: Start with Molotov

Note: This build requires Children of the Worm expansion for experimental gadgets.

DECK COMPOSITION (15 cards, in recommended order):
--------------------------------------------------
NOTE: All cards are active from the start of the level. Order matters for 
deck organization and understanding card priorities, but all effects are 
active immediately.

1. FOOD SCAVENGER (Fortune - Utility)
   - You can sense nearby Food Items
   - Adds a moderate amount of Food Items to the world
   - Food Items restore an additional 3 Health
   - Sustain through food
   - Active immediately

2. OUT WITH A BANG (Fortune - Utility)
   - When a teammate is incapacitated or dies, they drop an active Pipe Bomb
   - Emergency utility on teammate death
   - Active when teammate goes down

3. SUPPRESSING FIRE (Discipline - Offense)
   - Damaging Ridden with LMGs and Snipers has 40% chance to slow Ridden near target by 50% for 5 seconds
   - Crowd control utility
   - Active when using LMGs/Snipers

4. ULTRASONIC WOUND THERAPY (Brawn - Ability)
   - Heal 6 Health over 7 seconds to nearby teammates
   - Gadget Cost: 60 (Pistol/SMG ammo)
   - REPLACES: Offensive Slot
   - Team healing gadget
   - Active when used

5. EXPIRED T5 (Brawn - Ability)
   - Create a powerful blast that deals 20 Fire Damage to Weakspots
   - After initial blast, continue to deal 1 damage per second for 7 seconds
   - Gadget Cost: 75 (AR ammo)
   - REPLACES: Support Slot
   - Powerful offensive gadget
   - Active when used

6. EXPERIMENTAL STUN GUN (Reflex - Ability)
   - Enhance Bash to stun nearby enemies
   - Refreshes after 10 seconds
   - Gadget Cost: 30 (Pistol/SMG ammo)
   - REPLACES: Quick Slot, FRAGILE
   - Crowd control gadget
   - Active when bash is used

7. WELL RESTED (Brawn - Utility)
   - +20% Team Overheal
   - +5% Team Healing Efficiency
   - Team healing support
   - Active immediately

8. COPPER SCAVENGER (Fortune - Utility)
    - You can sense nearby Copper
    - More Copper Piles spawn
    - Copper generation for purchasing items/upgrades
    - Active immediately
    - NOTE: Replaced Stealthy Passage (which disables Quick Slot) to avoid conflict with Experimental Stun Gun

9. SADIST (Brawn - Offense)
    - When a teammate within 15 meters receives 5 or more damage, gain 20% of the damage as Temporary Health
    - Can only trigger once per second
    - Sustain through teammate damage
    - Active when teammate takes damage

10. AMMO SCAVENGER (Discipline - Utility)
    - You can sense nearby Ammo
    - 1 Additional Ammo crate spawn
    - Ammo generation (critical for gadgets)
    - Active immediately

11. DOWN IN FRONT! (Discipline - Defense)
    - While crouching you neither take nor deal Friendly Fire damage
    - +10 Health
    - ESSENTIAL for multiplayer - prevents friendly fire
    - Active when crouching

12. AMMO POUCH (Discipline - Offense)
    - +25% Ammo Capacity
    - +2.5% Bullet Damage
    - Ammo capacity for gadgets
    - Active immediately

13. LARGE CALIBER ROUNDS (Discipline - Offense)
   - +7.5% Bullet Damage
   - +200% Bullet Penetration
   - Damage and penetration for mutations
   - Active immediately

14. COMBAT TRAINING (Discipline - Offense)
   - +5% Bullet Damage
   - +1.5 Bullet Stumble Damage
   - +7.5 Melee Stumble Damage
   - Damage boost and stumble utility
   - Active immediately

15. AMMO BELT (Discipline - Offense)
    - +50% Ammo Capacity
    - +15% Reload Speed
    - Maximum ammo capacity for gadget usage
    - Active immediately

- Ultrasonic Wound Therapy: Heal 6 Health over 7 seconds to nearby teammates (Cost: 60 Pistol/SMG ammo, replaces Offensive Slot)
- Expired T5: 20 Fire Damage to Weakspots + 1 damage/sec for 7 seconds (Cost: 75 AR ammo, replaces Support Slot)

SPECIAL EFFECTS:

- Sense nearby Food Items (Food Scavenger)
- Food Items restore +3 Health (Food Scavenger)
- Sense nearby Ammo (Ammo Scavenger)
- +1 Ammo crate spawn (Ammo Scavenger)
- Sense nearby Copper (Copper Scavenger) - generates copper for upgrades
- 40% chance to slow Ridden near target by 50% for 5 seconds (Suppressing Fire with LMGs/Snipers)

PLAYSTYLE:
----------

Experimental tech specialist using DLC gadgets. Your gadgets replace item slots but cost ammo instead of consumables. Expired T5 is your main offensive tool (20 fire damage to weakspots + 1 damage/sec for 7 seconds). Ultrasonic Wound Therapy provides team healing (6 health over 7 seconds). Experimental Stun Gun gives crowd control (bash stuns enemies) but makes you fragile. Use Ammo Scavenger and high ammo capacity (+75%) to fuel your gadgets. Copper Scavenger generates copper for upgrades and item purchases. Large Caliber Rounds and Combat Training provide additional damage for your weapons. Your goal: use experimental tech to support the team while managing ammo carefully as gadgets are expensive.

STRENGTHS:
----------

- Powerful experimental gadgets (3 gadgets covering offense, support, and utility)
- Team utility through healing (Ultrasonic Wound Therapy)
- High ammo capacity for gadget usage (+75%)
- Good weapon damage (+15% bullet damage, +200% penetration)
- Copper generation (Copper Scavenger - sense and spawn more copper)
- Temp health generation (Sadist)
- Food sustain (Food Scavenger)

WEAKNESSES:
-----------

- Requires Children of the Worm expansion
- Gadgets are expensive (ammo cost)
- Replaces item slots (no normal offensive/support/quick items)
- Experimental Stun Gun makes you FRAGILE
- Requires ammo management
- Less conventional than standard builds
