TESLA - EXPERIMENTAL

Difficulty: No Hope
Weapons: LMGs or Snipers (for Suppressing Fire utility and ammo efficiency)
Playstyles: support

This deck is optimized for high-difficulty gameplay using experimental DLC technology in multiplayer. Focus: Experimental gadgets, ammo generation, team buffs, and advanced tech utilization.

Note: This build requires Children of the Worm expansion for experimental gadgets.


RECOMMENDED CLEANERS

PRIMARY: EVANGELO
- BORN SURVIVOR: Can break out of grabs, +75% Breakout Speed
- LIGHT FOOTED (Team): +5% Team Movement Speed
- FIT: +25% Stamina Regen
- FIRESTARTER: Start with Molotov


DECK COMPOSITION (15 cards)

1. FOOD SCAVENGER (Fortune - Utility)
   You can sense nearby Food Items. Adds a moderate amount of Food Items to the world. Food Items restore an additional 3 Health.

2. OUT WITH A BANG (Fortune - Utility)
   When a teammate is incapacitated or dies, they drop an active Pipe Bomb.

3. SUPPRESSING FIRE (Discipline - Offense)
   Damaging Ridden with LMGs and Snipers has 40% chance to slow Ridden near target by 50% for 5 seconds.

4. ULTRASONIC WOUND THERAPY (Brawn - Ability)
   Heal 6 Health over 7 seconds to nearby teammates. Gadget Cost: 60 (Pistol/SMG ammo). REPLACES: Offensive Slot.

5. EXPIRED T5 (Brawn - Ability)
   Create a powerful blast that deals 20 Fire Damage to Weakspots. After initial blast, continue to deal 1 damage per second for 7 seconds. Gadget Cost: 75 (AR ammo). REPLACES: Support Slot.

6. EXPERIMENTAL STUN GUN (Reflex - Ability)
   Enhance Bash to stun nearby enemies. Refreshes after 10 seconds. Gadget Cost: 30 (Pistol/SMG ammo). REPLACES: Quick Slot, FRAGILE.

7. WELL RESTED (Brawn - Utility)
   +20% Team Overheal. +5% Team Healing Efficiency.

8. COPPER SCAVENGER (Fortune - Utility)
   You can sense nearby Copper. More Copper Piles spawn.

9. SADIST (Brawn - Offense)
   When a teammate within 15 meters receives 5 or more damage, gain 20% of the damage as Temporary Health. Can only trigger once per second.

10. AMMO SCAVENGER (Discipline - Utility)
    You can sense nearby Ammo. 1 Additional Ammo crate spawn.

11. DOWN IN FRONT! (Discipline - Defense)
    While crouching you neither take nor deal Friendly Fire damage. +10 Health. ESSENTIAL for multiplayer.

12. AMMO POUCH (Discipline - Offense)
    +25% Ammo Capacity. +2.5% Bullet Damage.

13. LARGE CALIBER ROUNDS (Discipline - Offense)
    +7.5% Bullet Damage. +200% Bullet Penetration.

14. COMBAT TRAINING (Discipline - Offense)
    +5% Bullet Damage. +1.5 Bullet Stumble Damage. +7.5 Melee Stumble Damage.

15. AMMO BELT (Discipline - Offense)
    +50% Ammo Capacity. +15% Reload Speed.

EXPERIMENTAL GADGETS

Ultrasonic Wound Therapy
Heal 6 Health over 7 seconds to nearby teammates. Cost: 60 Pistol/SMG ammo. Replaces Offensive Slot.

Expired T5
20 Fire Damage to Weakspots + 1 damage/sec for 7 seconds. Cost: 75 AR ammo. Replaces Support Slot.

Experimental Stun Gun
Bash stuns nearby enemies, refreshes after 10 seconds. Cost: 30 Sniper Rifle ammo. Replaces Quick Slot, makes you FRAGILE.


PLAYSTYLE

Experimental tech specialist using DLC gadgets. Your gadgets replace item slots but cost ammo instead of consumables. Expired T5 is your main offensive tool for mutations and bosses. Ultrasonic Wound Therapy provides team healing. Experimental Stun Gun gives crowd control but makes you fragile. Use Ammo Scavenger and high ammo capacity (+75%) to fuel your gadgets. Copper Scavenger generates copper for upgrades. Large Caliber Rounds and Combat Training provide additional damage for your weapons. Your goal: use experimental tech to support the team while managing ammo carefully as gadgets are expensive.


STRENGTHS

- Powerful experimental gadgets (3 gadgets covering offense, support, and utility)
- Team utility through healing
- High ammo capacity for gadget usage (+75%)
- Good weapon damage (+15% bullet damage, +200% penetration)
- Copper generation
- Temp health generation (Sadist)
- Food sustain


WEAKNESSES

- Requires Children of the Worm expansion
- Gadgets are expensive (ammo cost)
- Replaces item slots (no normal offensive/support/quick items)
- Experimental Stun Gun makes you FRAGILE
- Requires constant ammo management
- Less conventional than standard builds
