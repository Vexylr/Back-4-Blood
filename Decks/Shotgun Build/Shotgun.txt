SHOTGUN - AA12 SPECIALIST

Difficulty: No Hope
Weapons: AA12 (primary), Magnum (offhand)
Playstyles: dps, front liner

This deck is optimized for high-difficulty shotgun gameplay using the AA12. Focus: High damage, damage resistance, trauma resistance, and shotgun sustain.


RECOMMENDED CLEANERS

PRIMARY: HOLLY
- CARDIO: +25 Team Stamina
- TOUGH AS NAILS (Team): +5% Team Damage Resistance
- BATTLE LUST: Melee kills heal 2 Health
- Starts with Bat

Why Holly: Guide recommends Holly on shotguns (preferably AA12). With the right cards, you can intelligently use her health regeneration ability and convert it to temporary health. Her melee sustain synergizes with shotgun gameplay.

PLAYSTYLE

Shotgun specialist focusing on AA12 with hip-fire accuracy. Glass Cannon provides +25% damage but reduces health (-30%), making positioning critical. Shell Carrier and Rolling Thunder provide +20% shotgun damage, combined with Glass Cannon for +45% base damage. Confident Killer stacks up to +15% more damage from mutation kills. Ridden Slayer provides +20% weakspot damage for No Hope. High trauma resistance (50%) and sustain cards keep you durable. Shell Carrier provides +30% shotgun ammo capacity. Rolling Thunder gives +35% movement speed while firing. Scattergun Skills provides +40% shotgun reload speed, combined with Mag Coupler for +90% total reload speed. Crouch when firing for better pellet grouping (Hunker Down provides +40% accuracy and +10% damage resistance while crouched). Buckshot Bruiser provides temp health from shotgun hits (0.25 per pellet). Amped Up provides team healing during hordes. Use Magnum as offhand for mutations. Learn to aim and position properly - no Down In Front for friendly fire protection.


STRENGTHS

- Excellent damage output (+45% base, up to +60% with Confident Killer) - essential for No Hope
- Weakspot damage (+20%) for mutation elimination
- Excellent trauma resistance (50%)
- Good sustain through Buckshot Bruiser temp health, food, and team healing
- Very fast reload speed (+90% total)
- High hip-fire accuracy (+50% base, +90% while crouching)
- Movement speed while firing (+35%) for kiting
- Shotgun ammo capacity (+30%)
- With Holly: Health regeneration from melee kills + team stamina and damage resistance support


WEAKNESSES

- Low health pool (-20% net from Glass Cannon) - positioning critical
- No fire resistance (-100%)
- Needs to find AA12 early for optimal performance
- DISABLES: Aim Down Sights (hip-fire only)
- Requires crouching for optimal defense
