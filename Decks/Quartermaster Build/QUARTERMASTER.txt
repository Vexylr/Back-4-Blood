QUARTERMASTER - SUPPLIER

Difficulty: No Hope
Weapons: LMGs (ALL IN on LMGs for Suppressing Fire synergy)
Playstyles: support, looter

This deck is optimized for high-difficulty support and resource generation in multiplayer. Focus: Ammo generation, item scavenging, team resource management, and utility support.


RECOMMENDED CLEANERS

PRIMARY: HOFFMAN
- ALWAYS PREPARED: Chance to find ammo or Offensive Accessories when killing Ridden
- CARGO POCKETS (Team): +15% Max Team Ammo Capacity
- READY TO ROCK: +1 Offensive Inventory
- NEVER WITHOUT: Start with Ammo Pack

ALTERNATIVE: HENG
- SCAVENGER SENSES: Can sense nearby Food and Cards
- PERSONAL CHEF: More Food spawns, gains same effects when teammates eat food
- COMFORT FOOD: Restores an additional 1 Trauma when consuming Food
- HIGH CARB DIET (Team): +1 Health and Stamina for each Food consumed
- I.E.D: Start with Pipe Bomb
- Note: Available in Tunnels of Terror expansion

PLAYSTYLE

Mobile supplier and resource generator. Your job is to keep the team stocked with ammo, weapons, and items. Use all your scavenger cards to find and generate resources throughout each level. Stay mobile with Cross Trainers, help with damage using your weapon cards, and support the team through resource generation. With Hoffman, provide massive ammo support (+50% team ammo capacity) and generate offensive items through kills. Bounty Hunter provides copper (up to 300 per level) for team upgrades. Avenge the Fallen gives emergency power (30% damage, 20% reload, unlimited ammo for 10 seconds) when teammates go down. Saferoom Recovery provides team sustain at level start.


STRENGTHS

- Multiple scavenger abilities (Ammo, Weapon, Offensive, Copper) for complete resource generation
- Massive team ammo support (+50% team capacity with Hoffman)
- Copper generation for upgrades (up to 300 per level)
- Weapon and item generation ensures team is well-equipped
- Emergency team buffs (Avenge the Fallen)
- Good damage output while supporting
- Team sustain at level start (Saferoom Recovery)


WEAKNESSES

- Lower personal health pool (+15 health only)
- Requires active scavenging to maximize effectiveness
- Less focused than specialized builds
- Team-dependent for optimal utility
- Moderate damage output (support role, not primary DPS)
